Frequently Asked Questions

What conditions does physiotherapy treat?

We treat back pain, sports injuries, post-surgical recovery, neurological conditions, arthritis, sciatica, frozen shoulder, and 40+ more.

Do I need a doctor's referral?

No referral needed. Book directly with us. Bring medical reports if you have any.

How many sessions will I need?

Varies by condition. Most patients see improvement within 8-16 sessions.
